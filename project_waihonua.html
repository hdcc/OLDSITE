Waihonua Drilled Shafts & Augered Cast In Place Piles

HDCC implemented auger-cast piles at the parking garage and drilled shaft caissons and sheet piles for the building foundation. Over 8,000 linear feet of drilled shafts were installed along with 10,000 linear feet of auger-cast piles at the 43-story urban condominium project.

This methodology of foundation work provides structural integrity while eliminating the normal noise and vibration caused by traditional precast concrete pilings, an important factor considering the close proximity to four other high rise condominiums on the block.

HDCC’s Waterfront and Foundations Division continues to adapt to industry trends and new techniques, leading the way in deep foundation work in Hawaii.
