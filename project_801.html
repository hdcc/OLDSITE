801 South Street Condominium

Located only 1.5 miles from Ala Moana Beach Park, this 46 story condominium tower addresses the critical housing shortage within Honolulu’s urban core.

801 South Street strives to fulfill the State’s mission to revitalize Kakaako with “workforce housing”, targeted for Hawaii’s locals not able to purchase homes due to the disparity between wages and the cost of homes.

Hawaiian Dredging is pleased to be part of creating an affordable "live, work and play" community for Hawaii’s working families.
