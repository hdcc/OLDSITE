Pulama Honua Newsletter

Pulama Honua is Hawaiian Dredging Construction Company's in-house newsletter concerned with exploring and sharing issues related to sustainable building in Hawaii.

In Hawaiian, "Pulama" means to "treasure, care for and cherish." It carries the connotation of a deep and profound respect.

In Hawaiian, "Honua" means "earth or planet" in a large context. The concept of Honua connects us to the interdependence of all life forms and activities on the planet.

The Pulama Honua Newsletter is published regularly throughout the year with updates on projects, sustainable construction methods, and green materials.
