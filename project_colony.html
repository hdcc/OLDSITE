Colony at the Peninsula

The Colony provides luxury condominium housing in the densely populated, coveted region of Hawaii Kai. With over 300 air conditioned units plus parking facilities, the development includes a rec center, pool, spa, and open-air pavillion.

Honolulu is growing at a rapid pace and accommodating new residents is increasingly a priority for developers and city planners. A source of revenue for the county and state, new residents are often looking for desirable homes close to the beach and city.

Hawaiian Dredging designed and built the post-tension concrete units for which we received an "outstanding" rating by the owner. Each unit features a central AC system and detailed finish work: wood trim, granite countertops, tiled baths, carpeting, and more. Other features include roads, sewage, drainage, water, fire lines, chiller plants, and utility lines to service the residence.

At HDCC we know what a home should be, and we strive to add a touch of aloha to each one we build. We're proud to have made another first-class condo that puts a roof over 300+ heads.
