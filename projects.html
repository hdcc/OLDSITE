The Hawaiian Department of Transportation asked for a minor miracle. They got the Kealakaha bridge. Weighing more than 1600 tons, the bridge crosses a ravine two football fields long and almost 200 feet deep. A marvel of engineering and teamwork. The first of its kind in Hawaii.

Pearl City Force Main Repair

Hawaiian Dredging performed an emergency repair on a ruptured quarter-mile stretch of the Pearl City Force sewer main. A first for the state, HDCC implemented a slip lining procedure to keep the main servicable for the next 20 years when the rest of the main will be replaced.
