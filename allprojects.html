Joint Traffic Management Center

An award-winning parking facility issued as a design-build competition in 2010.

See It
Map It: Kakaako learn more

The JTMC houses 410 parking stalls and a bus staging transit facility for 16 buses. This landmark structure is located within the Hawaii Capital Special District and was designed to complement and be compatible with the State's aesthetic and architectural heritage. The exterior façade includes perforated horizontal louver blades, architecturally formed patterned Hawaiian concrete walls, a large entry canopy and hanging planters.

801 South Street Condominium

Located only 1.5 miles from Ala Moana Beach Park, this 46 story condominium tower addresses the critical housing shortage within Honolulu’s urban core.

801 South Street strives to fulfill the State’s mission to revitalize Kakaako with “workforce housing”, targeted for Hawaii’s locals not able to purchase homes due to the disparity between wages and the cost of homes. Hawaiian Dredging is pleased to be part of creating an affordable "live, work and play" community for Hawaii’s working families.

Country Club Village 6

A 17-story condominium 269 living units and 514 parking stalls conveniently located in the Salt Lake district of Honolulu. The Village provides accessible housing for workers who commute to the city.

Hawaiian Dredging worked with the developer in designing this building to incorporate a cost-effective “tunnel forming” system which allows the concrete walls and suspended decks to be poured simultaneously. Utilizing this unique forming system from France, along with our global sourcing expertise in purchasing interior finish building materials, Hawaiian Dredging was able to offer the developer a quality, cost-effective solution to Hawaii’s housing market.
