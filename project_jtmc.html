Joint Traffic Management Center

An award-winning parking facility issued as a design-build competition in 2010.

The JTMC houses 410 parking stalls and a bus staging transit facility for 16 buses. This landmark structure is located within the Hawaii Capital Special District and was designed to complement and be compatible with the State's aesthetic and architectural heritage.

The exterior façade includes perforated horizontal louver blades, architecturally formed patterned Hawaiian concrete walls, a large entry canopy and hanging planters.
