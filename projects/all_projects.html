Aulani, A Disney Resort & Spa

Magic—that is the story of Disney's Aulani. A palatial entrance welcomes visitors to this 21 acre beachfront property.

Hundreds of luxury villas and hotel rooms, plus a water feature extravaganza including a saltwater snorkeling lagoon, a majestic mountain volcano with winding waterslides, and a lazy river that flows astride the Hawaiian landscape. Aulani is a showpiece of masterful artwork and engineering, richly decorated in a style celebrating Hawaii's culture and traditions.

Perfectly positioned on the pristine beaches of Ko Olina, Aulani offers seemingly endless recreation options. This worldrenowned resort and spa is the first of its kind for Disney and the largest for the state of Hawaii.

801 South Street Condominium

Located only 1.5 miles from Ala Moana Beach Park, this 46 story condominium tower addresses the critical housing shortage within Honolulu’s urban core.

801 South Street strives to fulfill the State’s mission to revitalize Kakaako with “workforce housing”, targeted for Hawaii’s locals not able to purchase homes due to the disparity between wages and the cost of homes. Hawaiian Dredging is pleased to be part of creating an affordable "live, work and play" community for Hawaii’s working families.
