Country Club Village 6

A 17-story condominium 269 living units and 514 parking stalls conveniently located in the Salt Lake district of Honolulu. The Village provides accessible housing for workers who commute to the city.

Hawaiian Dredging worked with the developer in designing this building to incorporate a cost-effective “tunnel forming” system which allows the concrete walls and suspended decks to be poured simultaneously.

Utilizing this unique forming system from France, along with our global sourcing expertise in purchasing interior finish building materials, Hawaiian Dredging was able to offer the developer a quality, cost-effective solution to Hawaii’s housing market.
